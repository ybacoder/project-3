Student Success

Leverage machine learning to predict your high school success.

We've created a questionnaire based on machine learning to predict your future high shcool success. Our model is based on the data from the Educational Longitudinal Study of 2002 (ELS: 2002). This dataset contains information on thousands of high school students and the parameters that shaped their high school, college, and professional careers. How did we do it?

  • Maching Learning Model: Random Forest Classifier with Imbalanced Learn Random Over Sampler
  • Predictive Questionnaire: 31 easy questions based on the ELS: 2002 dataset

What will will we predict?

Take our questionnaire to predict your future success in high school. What will your grade point average (GPA) be?
